Healthy Plate

Eating a balanced diet is essential for maintaining good health. A healthy plate should include a variety of food groups, including fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats.

Benefits of a Balanced Diet

Consuming a variety of nutrients from different food groups helps support overall health and can reduce the risk of chronic diseases.

A healthy plate gives our body the energy to think, play, study, and benefit us.THe plate will include all sorts of veggies,fruits,and other nutritious foods.

For more information on healthy eating,visit MyPlate.

Eat all kinds of foods

It's important to include a variety of foods in your diet to ensure you're getting all the nutrients your body needs.

More about healthy eating can be found at Nutrition.gov.

extra Nutrition Tips

Just split every nutrition item into parts and eat them in small amounts. This will help you to get all the nutrients you need without overeating. For example, instead of eating a whole apple, try slicing it into smaller pieces and eating it throughout the day.
