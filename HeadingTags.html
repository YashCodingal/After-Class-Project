Welcome to Facts about octopuses!

Amazing escape artists

An octopus can squeeze through any opening larger than its beak, the only hard part of its body. They've been known to escape aquariums and return to the ocean through surprisingly small gaps.

🎭 Masters of camouflage

They can change both color and skin texture in a fraction of a second. Some species can mimic rocks, coral, seaweed, and even other animals.
